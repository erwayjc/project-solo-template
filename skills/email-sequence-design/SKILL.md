## Email Sequence Design Playbook

### Common Sequence Types

**Welcome Series** (3-5 emails over 7-14 days)
1. Immediate: Welcome + deliver lead magnet + set expectations
2. Day 2: Share your story / origin + connect on values
3. Day 4: Provide high-value content related to their interest
4. Day 7: Soft introduction to your product/service
5. Day 10-14: Clear CTA with social proof

**Post-Purchase** (3-4 emails over 14 days)
1. Immediate: Order confirmation + what to expect
2. Day 2-3: Getting started guide / quick wins
3. Day 7: Check-in + advanced tips
4. Day 14: Request review/testimonial + referral ask

**Re-Engagement** (3 emails over 7 days)
1. "We miss you" + reminder of value
2. Exclusive offer or new content highlight
3. Final notice: "Should we stop emailing?"

### Timing Best Practices
- Welcome email: send immediately (within 5 minutes)
- B2C: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient local time
- Minimum 2 days between sequence emails
- Allow 24-48 hours after welcome before starting nurture

### Subject Line Framework
- Use curiosity gaps: "The one thing most creators get wrong about..."
- Use numbers: "3 strategies that doubled our conversion rate"
- Keep under 50 characters for mobile preview
- Personalize with first name in subject (sparingly)
- A/B test subject lines on broadcasts before automating

### Key Metrics to Track
- Open rate: benchmark 20-30%
- Click rate: benchmark 2-5%
- Unsubscribe rate: should be < 0.5% per email
- Sequence completion rate: aim for 60%+
